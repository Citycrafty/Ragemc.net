RAGE MC - DISCORD RULES

1.General Rules
1 Respect Everyone – Treat all members with kindness and respect. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination.
2 No NSFW Content – This includes images, gif, videos, text, or links containing explicit, sexual, or disturbing content.
3 No Spamming – Avoid excessive messages, emojis, mentions, or promotions.
4 No Self-Promotion or Advertising – Do not promote personal content (Twitch, YouTube, etc.) without permission in discord.
5 Keep Conversations Civil – Avoid political, religious, or otherwise controversial discussions unless specified.
2. Chats & Voice Rules
1 Use the Correct Channels – Follow the purpose of each channel and keep discussions relevant.
2 No Excessive Swearing – Mild swearing is okay, but don’t be excessive or offensive.
3 No Trolling or Disruptive Behavior – Don't intentionally annoy or disrupt conversations.
4 No Soundboards or Voice Changers – In meeting voice channels, use a clear mic and avoid unnecessary noise.
3. Moderation & Security
1 Follow Discord’s Terms of Service – Any violation of Discord’s ToS will result in removal.
2 No Impersonation – Do not pretend to be another member or staff.
3 No Sharing Personal Information – Protect your privacy and the privacy of others.
4 Listen to Staff & Mods – Their decisions are final; if you have concerns, discuss them respectfully.
